This gene set focuses on autosomal recessive inborn errors of metabolism characterized by mitochondrial respiratory chain deficiencies, lysosomal storage diseases, congenital disorders of glycosylation, fatty acid oxidation defects, and amino acid metabolism disorders. Clinical phenotypes commonly include developmental delay, intellectual disability, seizures, hypotonia, ataxia, optic atrophy, peripheral neuropathy, and multisystem involvement such as cardiomyopathy, hepatomegaly, renal dysfunction, and skeletal abnormalities. Many present in infancy or early childhood with progressive neurodegeneration, metabolic crises, or failure to thrive.
