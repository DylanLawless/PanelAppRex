- Dysregulation of cytokine signaling pathways, including interferon and interleukin receptors and downstream JAK-STAT signaling, contributing to immune dysregulation and inflammatory bowel disease.

- Complement system dysregulation leading to increased susceptibility to infections and inflammatory complications.
